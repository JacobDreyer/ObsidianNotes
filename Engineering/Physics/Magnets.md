## Magnets
#### Permanent Magnets
- Magnets always have 2 poles
	- A north pole
	- And a south pole
- like poles repel
- opposite poles attract
- Earth's georaphical north pole is actually a magnetic south pole

Like [[electric charge]]s create an [[electric field]]; magnets create a [[Magnetic Field]]
