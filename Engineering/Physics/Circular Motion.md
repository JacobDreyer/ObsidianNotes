## Circular Motion
- If we look at circular motion from the side it is Simple Harmonic Motion

- For example:
$$ x = R\cos(\omega t) $$
$$ y = R\cos(\omega t - \frac\pi2) $$
- $x$, $y$ = location
- $R$ = Radius of motion
- $\omega$ = [[Angular Frequency]]
