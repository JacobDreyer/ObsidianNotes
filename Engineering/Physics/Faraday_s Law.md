## Faraday's Law
- [[Faraday's Law]]: magnitude of [[Electromotive Force]] induced by a change in [[Magnetic Field]]/[[Magnetic Flux]]

$$ \epsilon = -\frac{\Delta\Phi_B}{\Delta t} $$
- $\epsilon$ = [[Electromotive Force]](EMF)
- $\Phi_B$ = [[Magnetic Flux]]
- $t$ = time
