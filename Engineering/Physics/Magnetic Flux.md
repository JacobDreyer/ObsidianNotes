## Magnetic Flux
$$ \Phi_B = \vec{B}\cdot\vec{A} = BA\cos\theta = \int\vec{B}\cdot d\vec A $$
- $B$ = [[Magnetic Field]]
- $A$ = Area of plane surface
- $\Phi_B$ = Magnetic Flux
- $\theta$ = angle between vector perpindicular to surface and Magnetic Field
