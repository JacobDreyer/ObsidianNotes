## Mass-[[Springs]] System
- Consisting of a mass and a spring

#### [[Angular Frequency]]:
- angular frequency of a mass spring system:
$$ \omega = \sqrt{\frac km} $$
- $\omega$ = [[Angular Frequency]]
- $k$ = Spring Constant
- $m$ = mass on spring
