## Magnetic Moment
#### Magnetic Dipole Moment
$$ \vec\mu = IN\vec A $$
- $I$ = Current through loop
- $N$ = Number of loops
- $\vec A$ = Magnitude of Area in direction out of loop
- $\vec\mu$ = Magnetic Dipole Moment. Same direction as $\vec A$
