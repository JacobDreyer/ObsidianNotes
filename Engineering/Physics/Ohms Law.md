## Ohm's Law
$$ J = \sigma E $$
- $J$ = [[Current]] density
- $\sigma$ = conductivity
- $E$ = [[Electric Field]]

$$ \Delta V = IR $$
- $I$ = [[Current]]
- $R$ = [[Resistance]]
- $\Delta V$ = [[Potential Difference]](Voltage)
