## Wave Intensity
$$ I = \frac PA $$
- $I$ = Wave Intensity
- $P$ = [[Wave Power]]
- $A$ = Area

Sound Wave Intensity
$$ I = \frac P{4\pi r^2} $$
- $I$ = Wave Intensity
- $P$ = [[Wave Power]]
- $r$ = distance from origin of sound
