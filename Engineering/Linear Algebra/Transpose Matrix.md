## Transpose of a Matrix
$$ A^T $$
- Rows -> Columns
- Columns -> Rows
- Ex:
$$
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 & -1 \\
3 & -5 & 6 
\end{pmatrix}
\to
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 3 \\
2 & -5 \\
-1 & 6
\end{pmatrix}
$$
- Order:
	- Row 1 -> Column 1
	- Row 2 -> Column 2
	- $\cdots$
