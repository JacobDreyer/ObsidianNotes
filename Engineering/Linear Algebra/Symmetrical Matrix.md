## Symmetrical Matrix
- type of [[Matrices]]
$$ A^T = A $$
- $A^T$ = [[Transpose Matrix]]
- if $A$ and $B$ are symmetric:
$$ A+B\qquad,\qquad A-B\qquad,\qquad k\cdot A\qquad, \qquad A^{-1} $$
- Are symmetric
- $A^{-1}$ = [[Matrix Inverse]]
