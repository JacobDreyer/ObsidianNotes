## Angles
#### Angles in Linear Algebra
$$ \langle u,v\rangle = 0 \quad \Rightarrow\quad u\perp v $$
- V is an [[Inner Products]] space
- W is a subspace of V
- $W^\perp =$ {$u\in V$ such that $\langle u,w\rangle = 0$ for any $w\in W$}
