## [[Identity Matrix]] (Unit Matrix) ($I_n$)
$$
I_3 = 
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
$$
- $n$ = column/row size
- All elements are 0 except those on the diagonal
- Matrix is square
- Note:
$$ A\cdot A^{-1} = I_n $$
