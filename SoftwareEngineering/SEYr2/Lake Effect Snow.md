Lake effect snow is caused by cold air moving over relatively warm water. Snowbelts are found downwind of the lakes (In winter the wind is often from the northwest)

![[Pasted image 20230305121853.png]]

### In [[Southern Ontario]]
[[London]] and [[Kitchener]] frequently recieve Lake effect snow from [[Lake Huron]] causing high annual snowfall

[[Windsor]] occasionally receives lake effect snow from [[Lake Michigan]]
