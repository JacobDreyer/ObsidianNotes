[[Petroleum]] deposits off the coast of [[St. John's]] are a source of optimisim for [[Newfoundland]]. 

In [[2007]], the province negotiated a 4.9% ownership share in the offshore drilling projects.

The revenue generated resulted in [[Newfoundland and Labrador]] becoming a "have" province in [[2009]] for the first time
