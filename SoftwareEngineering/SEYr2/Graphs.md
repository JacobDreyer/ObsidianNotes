##### Vertices:
$$ V = \{v_1, v_2, \cdots, v_n\} $$
##### Edges:
$$ E = \{w_1, w_2, \cdots, w_e\} $$
such that $w_i$ has 2 adjacent vertices: $v_i, v_j$

$$ G = (V, E) $$
