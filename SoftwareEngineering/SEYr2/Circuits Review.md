### Power Supplied in a Circuit:
$$ P = v_{batt}\cdot i_{batt} $$
#### Voltage Divider:
$$ v_1 = R_1i = \frac{R_1}{R_1+R_2+R_3}v_{total} $$
#### Current Divider:
$$ i_1 = i_{total}\frac{R_2}{R_1 + R_2} $$

## Capacitors:
#### Charge:
$$ q = Cv $$


## Inductors:
#### Current:
$$ i(t) = \frac1L\int_{0}^{t}v(t)dt + i(0) $$
#### In Series:
$$ L_{eq} = L_1 + L_2 + L_3 $$
