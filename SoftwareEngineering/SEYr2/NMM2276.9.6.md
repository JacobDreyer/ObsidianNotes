Find an equation of the [[Tangent Plane]] to the graph of the given equation at the indicated point
$$ x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 9 \qquad (-2,2,1) $$

$$ 2x_0(x-x_0) + 2y_0(y-y_0) + 2z_0(z-z_0) = 0 $$
$$ 2(-2)(x+2) + 2(2)(y-2)+2(1)(z-1) = 0 $$
$$ -4x-8 + 4y-8 + 2z-2 = 0 $$
$$ -4x+4y+2z = 18 $$
$$ -2x+2y+1 = 9 $$
