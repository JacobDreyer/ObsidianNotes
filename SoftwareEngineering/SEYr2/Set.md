### [[De Morgan's Law]]
$$ A, B, C, \subseteq U $$
$$ A\cap(B\cup C) = (A\cap B)\cup (A\cap C) $$
$$ A\cup (B\cap C) = (A\cup B)\cap(A\cup C) $$
$$ U/(A\cup B) = (U/A)\cap(U/B) $$
$$ U/(A\cap B) = (U/A)\cup (U/B) $$
