Over 80% of [[Quebec]]'s population lives in an urban area

Over 70% of the population live in one of 6 [[CMA]]s: 
- [[Montreal]]
- [[Quebec City]]
- [[Gatineau]]
- [[Sherbrooke]]
- [[Saguenay]]
- [[Trois-Rivieres]]

[[Saguenay]] is the name of two cities that merged in [[2002]] ([[Chicoutimi]] and [[Jonquiere]])
