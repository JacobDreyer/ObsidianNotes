## Categorical [[Variable]]
- places individuals into one of several groups or categories
- the values are labels for the different categories
- the distribution of a categorical variable lists the count or percent of individuals who fall into each catgory

- When a [[Data Set]] involves 2 categorical variables, we begin by examining the counts or percents in various catgories for one of the variables
